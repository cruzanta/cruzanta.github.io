About Me

Hi, I'm Anthony. I am a project coordinator in the market research industry by day and an inquisitive software developer by night. I hold a Bachelor of Arts in Computer Science with a minor in Electronics and Computer Engineering Technology from Bowling Green State University. In my free time, I enjoy developing software solutions to everyday problems, reading up on the latest technology trends, long distance running, and exploring the nature around me. I am always looking for new challenges to tackle and problems to solve. If you have a project that you think I would be interested in, feel free to get in touch.
